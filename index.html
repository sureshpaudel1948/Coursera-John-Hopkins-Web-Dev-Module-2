Vidya Tattvam Paddhati

Education

Education is that lowest part of 'ABSOLUTE KNOWLEDGE' under which we know, see and gain any thing or position in between the world or the Universe or Bramhand and the body. This is 'by the senses and of the senses' of the body. All such education-based achievements are upto the body only. Education is also called Shiksha in Sanskrit. Education is described as the means through which individuals come to understand, perceive, or attain things. It implies that education is the process through which we gain knowledge and insight into the world around us.

Self-Realization

Self Realization is the second system under 'ABSOLUTE KNOWLEDGE' under which we know, see and gain any thing and the position of Self. Self Realization is based on 'by the Self, for the Self and of the Self'. Self Realization is secondly called 'Swaddhyaya' in Sanskrit which means 'the study of the Self. Self Realization is also portrayed as a means to understand one's own position or nature. This goes beyond just acquiring knowledge about external things; it involves gaining deep insight into the self, one's identity, and one's place in the world.

Spiritualization

The System under which we know, see and gain any thing or position of the Soul is Yoga or Spiritualization. This is based on 'by the Soul, for the Soul and of the Soul. The Soul is not Perfect, hence Liberation and Immortality (Mukti and Amartaa) are not achieved under this system. Spiritualization is also known as 'Addhyatma' which means 'towards Aatma. Yoga or Spiritualization is inherently soul-centric. It is a process that is initiated, conducted, and oriented toward the soul itself. This highlights the deeply introspective and self-reflective nature of spiritual practices.
